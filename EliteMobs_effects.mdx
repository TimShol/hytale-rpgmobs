# Effects

Effects are configured in `effects.yml` under `byId`.

## Example: Projectile Resistance

```yaml
byId:
  projectile_resistance:
    amountMultiplierPerTier:
      - 0.0
      - 0.0
      - 0.0
      - 0.7
      - 0.85
    infinite: true
    isEnabledPerTier: [false, false, false, true, true]
```

Effects reference templates in `Server/Entity/Effects/EliteMobs`.
